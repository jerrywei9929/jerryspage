April 22, 2019

Under Armour Female Market Survey Analysis using R

In 2017 Under Armour tried to branch out to the female market in sports apparel by simply going "pink" was proven not successful. Since the company has mainly focused on making apparels for men, it lacked some knowledge in their female consumer market. The objective of this analysis is to find out if apparel consumption is driven by materialistic of hedonistic values, and how these values apply to the future purchase possibility of the product.
